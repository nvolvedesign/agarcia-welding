About A.Garcia Welding

We pride ourselves in offering our customers unique wrought iron designs. Each project contains a creative approach in style and elegance. We currently serve in the Inland Empire (Murrieta, Temecula, Lake-Elsinore, Perris, Fallbrook and Corona). Please take a look at our gallery or contact us here for more information.

With 8 years of experience, A.Garcia Welding provides customers with a variety of modern and traditional styles. We consider each piece a form of art and a representation of the client’s home.
